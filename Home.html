EcoTech

The "Eco-Tech" is a technology initiative that can identify natural hazards that may fall, which in this case are trees. In this regard, the project remains constructed as a real-time hazard map that preserves the natural environment while making sure that people are safe in this condition. It may prevent accidents, property damage, and damage to ecosystems.

develop a digital platform that identifies and marks potential hazards like leaning or dying trees on an interactive map, therefore providing early warnings and preventative measures through the accurate mapping of risky areas in urban and rural settings through citizen reporting integration, harnessing technology for environmental sustainability.



Project Features and Goals


  • **Community Participation:** It allows citizens to report suspect or hazardous trees under the platform.
  • **Risk Assessment:** Supplies analysis of data to prioritize the areas of concern based upon risk potential..
  • **Environmental Awareness:** Increases safety with no compromise in the environmental integrity
